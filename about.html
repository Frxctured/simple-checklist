About

Why did I make this?

I needed a simple, no-nonsense checklist application that just works. No ads, no tracking, just a clean space to get things sorted out. Everything is stored locally in your browser, keeping your data private, secure, and instantly accessible without annoying log-ins.

Design choice

The design is intentionally flat and minimalist. By stripping away heavy borders, shadows, and unnecessary colors, the interface stays out of your way and the focus remains entirely on the tasks at hand. It takes direct inspiration from clean, modern LLM chat interfaces, making it feel familiar and lightweight.
